The Moog Factory

Located on the northside of Asheville’s Broadway Street, the Moog factory is a staple within the local community. From the first stages of design and engineering to the production floor where every new Moog synthesizer is born, it all happens in the Moog factory.

Come visit and get hands on with every Moog synthesizer and discover the engineering and artistry behind the Moog sound.

Located in our factory building in downtown Asheville, North Carolina, the Moog Store offers free in-store presentations twice daily. These presentations focus on the history of our company and include demonstrations of our instruments and how they work. The sessions take place at 10:30am and 3:30pm and last about 45 minutes.

The Moog Store

Since opening 2011, the Moog Store has served as a public portal to the Moog factory, inviting anyone to come inside and get hands on with electronic sound.

To make the best use of its unique connection to the Moog factory, the Moog Store has remained a brick and mortar experience, offering free public workshops and performances, tours of the Moog factory, inside synth demonstrations, and the opportunity to take home your own Moog instrument direct from the Moog factory.

Visit the Moog Store today to discover the engineering and the artistry behind the Moog sound.
